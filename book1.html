Description :

Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone, also called Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone, the first novel in the immensely popular Harry Potter series by British writer J.K. Rowling. It was first published in Britain in 1997 and appeared in the United States.Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone is an enthralling start to Harry’s journey toward coming to terms with his past and facing his future.The story is about a boy named Harry Potter whose parents get killed & he is sent to his Uncle & Aunt’s house where he is tortured until he turns 11. They refrain from telling Harry about his scar on his head & magical powers he inherits. On Harry’s 11th birthday is taken away by Hagrid to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft & Wizardry. Harry is guarded by Professor Dumbledore throughout the story while Voldemort who killed Harry’s parents leaving behind the scar on Harry’s head is behind Harry, who was the only wizard surviving Voldemort’s attack & hence was eminent in Hogwarts.
